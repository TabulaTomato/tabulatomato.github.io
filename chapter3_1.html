Chapter 3

into the unknown

chapter 1 & 2 summary

Let me remind you of the events in the last few days. My mother Lena disappeared, but she left me her gem that gives special powers, as well as a series of clues to find her old friend Mary. Mary told me that we are, in fact, members of a society that was forced to leave their planet Nahukat when it was attacked and eventually destroyed by a parasite civilisation, the Lakis.

My parents and Mary were a part of a small crew, sent from the mothership, on a mission to find a habitable planet for the survivors of Nahukat. As they succeeded in finding Voyager that held information about Earth, their own vessel suffered serious damage during the docking. My father died while undocking the destroyed module from their ship, as his safety tether broke, and the module took him with it.

The rest of the crew landed on Earth, where I was born. They never managed to contact the mothership, and my mother decided to keep my origin a secret from me.

The search for my mother took me to Starchester, where I discovered that she was investigating mayor Bundy. He was involved in some fishy tax business with PEI, 'Petroleum Extraction Industries'. PEI polluted the environment with their oil wells in Starchester county, which led to the poisoning of the local population. I even found a restricted ward in the Starchester hospital, where the people who got sick were kept in secret, under the supervision of Dr Turner.

My quest took me to the base of PEI, where Ron and Mary, former members of my parents' crew, recognized the Laki symbol. So, the Lakis are behind all of this, and they are planning to colonize Earth just like they did with Nahukat before!
I took the appearance of a Laki Agent 27, with one of my special powers, and managed to enter the building.

setup

In this chapter, your map will consist of 9 parts, which you will put on the table during the game.

You start with part:

MAP01

so put it in the middle of the table, facing up. Put the rest of the map parts on the side, face down.

Take cards:

01

08

13

23

26

from the character/object deck and put them on the table facing up. Put the rest of the character/object deck on the side, face down.

Take location card:

LOC01

from the location deck and put it on the table facing up. Every time you open a location, you can take all of the cards and special objects whose numbers are written on it.

In this case, you should take card:

20

from the character/object deck and put it on it's provided slot on the location card LOC01 facing up.

You should also take card:

SPO01

from the special object deck and put it on the table facing up.

Put the rest of the location deck on the side, face down. In this chapter, you can go to new locations as usual, but instead of entering the address into the Digital Game Book input field, enter the door number. For example, if you want to see what's behind door 01, enter 'D01' in the input field and press enter.

Put the special object deck next to the other decks, face down.

You will be using the Digital Game Book during the game, so keep the computer/tablet/phone with this website open within reach. We recommend you to read aloud the text from the Digital Game Book if you are playing with more than one player. In this case, you can switch turns for reading as often as you wish.

There are a few puzzles in this chapter where a piece of paper and a pen could be useful, so keep them on the side. You can also always repeat any entry in the Digital Game Book to remember what the book said.
There is no time limit for playing the game, and there are no negative points for asking questions or making wrong combinations of objects in the game. So feel free to do whatever comes to your mind.

If you get stuck, use the hint system. But remember to examine all the cards you're supposed to, speak to all of the characters you meet in the game, and visit the rooms in the base.

Start the game by examining the cards you were given in the beginning.

IMPORTANT: You are starting this chapter disguised as Agent 27. You can use the usual input field having his appearance. When you want to change your appearance, use the power (card 26).

IMPORTANT: Chapter 3 consists of three parts. If you want to take a break, do it when you're finished with the first or second part.
If you're playing chapter 3 after a break and you've already reached part 2 of part 3 of the chapter in your previous play session, click on one of the buttons below.
Otherwise, continue with the Digital Game Book.

hints

Enter the character/object card number, or special object number you want a hint for.

For example, if you want a hint for the character card 01, enter 01 in the input field and click OK or press enter.
Or if you want a hint for the special object SPO01, enter SPO01 in the input field and click OK or press enter.
