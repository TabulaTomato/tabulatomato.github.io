about the game

Ziggy Wonder is a narrative puzzle mystery game in which you take the character of Ziggy Wonder. The game consists of three chapters, each providing about 2 hours of playtime. The goal is to discover the truth behind the secrets you will encounter during the game and to solve a series of puzzles to get there.

how to play

You can skip this part and immediately start with Chapter 1. The game will guide you in the beginning so you will learn how to play while playing it. But, if you have any doubts, you can always check our 'How to Play' page.
