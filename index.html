about the game

Ziggy is an adventure puzzle game in which you take the character of Ziggy Wonder. The game consists of three chapters, each providing for about 2 hours of playtime. The goal is to discover the truth behind the secrets you will encounter during the game and to solve a series of puzzles to get there.
