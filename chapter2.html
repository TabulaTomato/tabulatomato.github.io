Chapter 2

meeting the crew

chapter 1 summary

Well, my story is so strange that I have to pinch myself to make sure it's really happening. My mother Lena left town, explaining herself only with a short and confusing phone call, after which she turned the phone off. But what followed turned out to be even stranger. She left me a series of clues to find her old friend Mary as well as her gem that gave me a special power over plants and animals. In my search of Mary I discovered that my mother was seeing my university professor Martin. I also met a member of her environmentalist group 'Earthlings' and found out that my mother was doing research on something and was very secretive about it. But it was Mary who shared the most incredible piece of the puzzle with me. My mother, Mary and myself are not creatures of this Earth at all!
Our planet is called Nahukat. It was attacked and eventually destroyed by a parasite civilisation the Lakis and the rest of our people left the planet to find another habitable space body. My parents were in one of the search mission crews, along with Mary and two other astronauts. As they succeded to find and dock with Voyager, a spacecraft from Earth with information on the planet, their own vessel suffered serious damage in the process. My father sacrificed his life to save the ship.
The rest of the crew managed to reach Earth, where I was born. But as their communication system was broken, they weren't able to contact the mothership. After a couple of years of trying, my mother decided that it's best for me never to know the truth about my origin and to be peacefully raised as a human. She isolated herself from the rest of the crew and never told me anything. Until now.
Both Mary and I are certain that she did it for a reason and that she must be in some kind of danger. So we decided to figure out what's going on and save my mother!

chapter 2 introduction

The only lead we had was that my mother did some research in Starchester lately. Fortunately, we had great connections in the city.
Remember how there were five astronauts in their search mission crew? Mary was the captain and the main pilot of the ship; my mother Lena was the chief astronomer; my father Peku, the flight engineer; Rose, the physician and finally Ron, the technology specialist. While Mary and Lena chose Majorton for their new life, Rose and Ron lived in Starchester city.
Mary contacted them and told them what happened. Ron immediately thought of hacking my mother's bank account and found that she stayed in the 'Deep Lake Hotel' both times she visited Starchester in the last few months. Rose wanted to check out the hotel, but I insisted to go to the city at once. I couldn't just sit at home and do nothing, my mother was in danger! Mary stayed in Majorton in case that something shows up there, and I took the first bus to Starchester.
Equipped with a phone tracked to the crew's inner communication system - so that everybody could know where I am at all times, and the gem that still gave me my special power, I arrived in the morning and checked at the hotel.
I left my bag in the room, quickly freshened up and was just about to go to the lobby, when Mary called.
"Listen Ziggy, there's been a development. Professor Martin is missing. He didn't show up at the university since yesterday and he's not at home. His phone is dead, of course."
Professor Martin? There has to be a connection to what happened to my mother.
"Keep your eyes open. I'll call you if I hear something." said Mary and hanged up.
It's time to see if anybody from the hotel remembers my mother.

setup

Put the Starchester map on the board. Take cards 01, 08, 23, 28, 34 and LOC01 from the deck, without looking at the other cards. Put them on the provided spaces on the board, so that everybody can see them.
In this chapter, you will be also be using a special object deck, marked with capital letters SPO followed by a number. Put the special object deck next to the other two decks, face down.
You will be using the game book during the game, so keep the computer / tablet / phone with this website open within reach. It is recommended to read aloud the text from the game book if you are playing with more than one player. In this case, you can switch turns for reading as often as you wish.
You can keep a piece of paper and a pen on the side to note clues during the game, or you can always repeat any entry in the game book to remember what did the book say.
There is no time limit for playing the game, and there are no negative points for asking questions or making wrong combinations of objects in the game. So feel free to do what ever comes to your mind.
If you get stuck, use the hint system. But remember to examine all the cards that you're supposed to, speak to all of the characters you meet in the game and open every new location you were suggested to.
Start the game by examining the cards you were given in the beginning.
