Chapter 1

my mother's gone

prologue

It's always been just the two of us, a single mother and a child created with a little help of a sperm bank. I can't complain about it, I never felt that our family was missing a third person. I had a great childhood, living in our little bubble of happiness. However, my mother did seem a bit distant at times, as if living a peaceful life, taking care of me and stargazing on a clear night wasn't all that was on her mind. Sometimes she even used to say that she doesn't really belong here. But I never imagined that she could do something like this.
I'm still trying to comprehend what happened. Yesterday, when I came home from university the house was empty. I thought that she must have gone for a drink after work or something. I started to make dinner when I heard my phone ringing. It was her.
"Ziggy, I don't want you to worry, or to be mad at me. I had to leave.", she said. Before I managed to say something, she continued:
"This town was suffocating me. I know this seems a bit out of the blue, but I've been feeling this way for a very long time."
I went to her room and saw that her clothes were really gone.
"Mother, you're joking, right?", I asked in disbelief.
"You're all grown up now and you don't need me anymore. I need to have some time for me, to see what I really want from life. It's a lot to ask, but please understand. Take good care of yourself. I love you."I heard her voice breaking a bit, as if she was holding back tears.
"And don't forget to feed Laika!" was the last thing she said before hanging up. I tried to call her again but her phone was turned off.
I couldn't sleep all night. My mother had left me all alone. That's not like her at all. I can't believe that she would disappear like that, with nothing but a little goodbye over the phone. And what was this about feeding our dog? That's the last thing she wanted to say to me? Really? I just don't get it.

setup

Put the Majorton map on the table.
Take cards 01 and 23 from the character/object deck and put them on the on the table facing up. Put the rest of the character/object deck on the side, face down.
Take LOC01 from the location deck and put it on the table facing up. Every time you open a location, you can take all of the cards and special objects written on it. Put the rest of the location deck on the side, face down.
You will be using the Digital Game Book during the game, so keep the computer/tablet/phone with this website open within reach. It is recommended to read aloud the text from the Digital Game Book if you are playing with more than one player. In this case, you can take turns for reading as often as you wish.
You can keep a piece of paper and a pen on the side to note clues during the game, or you can always repeat any entry in the Digital Game Book to remember what the book said.
There is no time limit for playing the game, and there are no negative points for asking questions or making wrong combinations of objects in the game. So, feel free to do whatever comes to your mind.
If you get stuck, use the hint system. But remember to examine all the cards that you're supposed to, speak to all of the characters you meet in the game and open every new location you were suggested to.
Start the game by examining the location LOC01 you were given in the beginning.

IMPORTANT: To examine the location, enter 'LOC01' or 'loc01' in the Digital Game Book input field and click 'OK' or press Enter.

This is how you can examine a location every time you get a new one.
